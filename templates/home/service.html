Quantitative Services

Our quantitative analysis services leverage statistical methods to transform raw data into actionable insights. By meticulously examining numerical data, we uncover patterns, trends, and correlations that inform strategic decision-making.

Computer Assisted Telephonic Interview (CATI)

Trained interviewers conduct surveys over the phone using computer-assisted technology.

Pen And Paper Interview (PAPI)

Interviewers conduct face-to-face interviews using paper questionnaires.

Computer Assisted Personal Interview (CAPI)

Interviewers conduct face-to-face interviews using handheld devices or tablets.

Computer Assisted Web Interview (CAWI)

Respondents complete surveys online through a web browser.

Independent Quality Control

Maintain the highest standards of data integrity with our rigorous independent quality control processes. Our dedicated team ensures data accuracy, reliability, and adherence to industry best practices, providing you with confidence in your research outcomes.

Time Check

Verify the duration of interviews or surveys to ensure compliance with ethical guidelines and data accuracy.

Response Check

Validate the completeness and consistency of respondent answers to maintain data quality.

GPS Check

Confirm the geographic location of respondents to verify data accuracy and identify potential fraud.

Forensic Check

Detect patterns of inconsistent or suspicious responses to identify potential data anomalies.

Audio Listening

Review recorded interviews to assess interviewer performance, respondent behavior, and data quality.

Callback

Contact respondents to clarify responses, resolve issues, or obtain additional information.
